About Hip Hop Hack

Our goal for Hip-Hop Hacks is to explore how technology has immersed itself into the hip-hop and music community.

Technology and hip-hop are incredibly important to one another. From its roots, hip hop has been boosted by technology, from turntables and microphones to mediums like, vinyl, compact cassettes, mp3s, and now, apps and algorithms. We want to encourage youth to create technological innovations that will impact the future of music.
